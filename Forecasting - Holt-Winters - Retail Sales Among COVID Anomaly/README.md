# Forecasting – Holt-Winters – Retail Sales Among COVID Anomaly
*Forecasting in the presence of unprecedented disruption.*

This project develops a retail sales forecasting solution using Holt-Winters exponential smoothing techniques. Particular attention is given to the challenges posed by COVID-19 anomalies and their impact on seasonality, trends, and forecasting accuracy.

## Key Techniques
- Time series preprocessing
- Trend and seasonality analysis
- Holt-Winters forecasting
- Forecast evaluation
- Anomaly interpretation

## Business Impact
Accurate demand forecasting supports inventory planning, staffing decisions, and financial forecasting, even during periods of significant market disruption.
