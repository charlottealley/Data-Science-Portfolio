# Causal Inference – Uplift – Identifying Persuadable Voters with and without Social Media
*Measuring who can actually be influenced, not just predicted.*

This project compares uplift models across two datasets: one containing anonymized campaign information and another enhanced with social media embeddings derived from 2024 Presidential election tweets. The analysis focuses on identifying persuadable voters and evaluating whether additional text-derived information improves targeting performance.

## Key Techniques
- Uplift modeling
- Causal inference
- Text embeddings
- Model comparison and evaluation
- Data-driven policy and campaign recommendations

## Analytical Impact
Traditional predictive models estimate outcomes, while uplift models estimate treatment effects. This distinction enables more efficient allocation of resources and interventions.
